Why Martin Luther King Jr. is a hero

Martin Luther king Jr. was an American Baptist minister and activist who also became to be the most visible spokesperson and leader in the civil rights movement. He was an important voice of the American civil rights movement, which worked for equal rights for all. With all his given hard work he became the youngest person ever to receive a Nobel Peace Prize in 1964. Martin Luther King Jr. gave speeches which made him known more to many people and which made congress move faster in passing the Civil Rights Act. Martin Luther King Jr. is recognized has a hero for his nonviolent protests for freedom, peace, and equality between blacks and whites.
